About us

Welcome to Times Group of Institutions, a premier educational institution established in 1996 and dedicated to academic excellence for over two decades. As a unit of Times Educational Welfare Society and recognized by the Government of Telangana, we offer a comprehensive range of programs spanning SSC, Intermediate, Degree, Post-Graduate, Skill Education, and IT Education.

At Times Group of Institutions, we are committed to nurturing talent, fostering innovation, and empowering students to excel in their chosen fields. With a focus on holistic development, we provide a conducive learning environment, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities to ensure our students receive the best education possible.

Whether you're embarking on your academic journey or seeking to enhance your skills and knowledge, Times Group of Institutions is here to guide and support you every step of the way. Join us and unlock your potential for a bright and successful future.
